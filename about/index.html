Our story

Why we built this

The payment industry has a fragmentation problem. Most businesses either lock themselves into a single gateway and hope for the best — or spend months building brittle, bespoke integrations with multiple providers. Neither approach scales.

We built InfyniPay to change that. By creating PaymentHood — a unified payment orchestration engine — we give any business the ability to connect once and access every major gateway, with smart routing, failover, and real-time analytics built in.

We're a small, focused team operating out of Amsterdam. We care about doing this right: transparent pricing, clean APIs, and a product that's genuinely useful to the developers and operations teams who rely on it every day.

Backed by real-world scale. PaymentHood is a battle-tested platform already used by businesses worldwide to manage multiple payment gateways, reduce processing costs, and maximise transaction success rates.
